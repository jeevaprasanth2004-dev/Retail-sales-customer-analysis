Project Overview

This project analyzes retail sales transactions and customer purchasing behavior to identify business trends, customer value, and revenue growth opportunities. Using RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) Analysis, customers are segmented into meaningful groups such as Champions, Loyal Customers, Big Spenders, At Risk, and Others.

The project includes an interactive Power BI dashboard that provides insights into sales performance, customer segmentation, category performance, and monthly sales trends to support data-driven business decisions.

---

🎯 Objectives

- Analyze overall retail sales performance.
- Monitor month-wise sales trends.
- Perform customer segmentation using RFM analysis.
- Compare customer distribution across product categories.
- Measure business growth against the previous year.
- Generate actionable business recommendations.

---

📊 Dashboard Features

8. Prepared business insights and recommendations.

---

📈 Key Insights

- Revenue increased by 24.9% compared to the previous year.
- July recorded the highest sales and customer activity.
- Office Supplies had the largest customer base.
- Champions and Loyal Customers represent the strongest customer segments.
- At Risk customers require targeted retention campaigns.

---

💡 Business Recommendations

- Launch personalized campaigns for At Risk customers.
- Reward Champions and Loyal Customers with loyalty programs.
- Increase marketing during seasonal peak months.
- Improve customer purchase frequency.
- Cross-sell Technology products to Office Supplies customers.
